What does 20XX mean?

20XX (pronounced twenty exty ex) is a short hand for "some time in the future" that is often used in video games.

The first time I encountered it was while playing the Megaman game series. Then it was as 201X, then in a later game it would be 202X.

Since then, any references to "some time in the future", has been marked with 20XX. We can no longer rely on 202X as we are (at time of writing) more than a fifth of the way through.
Here's to the future! Here's to 21XX!
